Current Events

Every year for our club's second meeting, we like to celebrate the achievements of the previous year by holding a potluck at the 4-H camp in Monticello. There members are rewarded with certificates for year completion, perfect record books, new members, cloverbuds, and new officers. The leaders also presented each 4-H family with their choice of a blanket, portable chargers, gift card, coasters, and a spatula and cookie cutter. After the ceremony, members and their families were dismissed to go eat and the achievement celebration began. Each family brought a covered dish to share or drinks. Once everyone was full, our club's junior leader, Miranda Fairbanks, led the club in a series of team games that included relay races and using their resources to accomplish the tasks presented. Then, it was time for the spooky hayride. The members rode down to the old dining hall where they were greeted by several scarers. One of our club's leaders, Lori Gregg told a scary story. Overall, the event was a great success and super fun!

About Us

Our club, the Sadorus 4-H All-Stars was founded in the early 60s and is still continues to be the largest 4-H club in Champaign County.

Our Club's Mission:

Help out the Community, Work Hard, and Become the Best Version of Ourselves.
